I am a Data Engineer specializing in Financial Risk and Credit Analytics. I joined Porto Real Asset as the first data professional and built the entire data infrastructure from scratch - from setting up version control to production pipelines that deliver daily risk reports to the investment team.

My work spans the full data lifecycle: ingestion, transformation, storage, and delivery. I have built automated pipelines processing 1.5GB+ datasets, extraction systems for 5,000+ PDF documents, and ML models for credit risk explainability using SHAP.

I hold a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics from FGV EMAp, where my thesis focused on estimation of spatiotemporal Poisson processes with missing location data.

My technical stack includes Python, SQL, Apache Airflow, Docker, and Linux. I have domain expertise in FIDCs (credit funds), fixed income pricing, and risk analysis.
